Our Philosophy

Cooking is
an act of love

Maison Dorée was born from a simple dream: to create a space where French culinary tradition could be celebrated in its most authentic form. Founded by Pierre and Marguerite Laurens, our restaurant opened its doors in the heart of the city in 1994 with just twelve tables and an unshakeable belief in excellence.

Today, under the stewardship of their daughter and head chef, Éloise Martel, the same spirit endures. We have earned three Michelin stars, yet we remain fundamentally unchanged — driven by a love for the craft and deep respect for our guests.
